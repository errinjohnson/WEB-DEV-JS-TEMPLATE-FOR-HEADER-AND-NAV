How do we increase the number of women in technology? How do we retain and close the gender gap? How can we encourage young girls interested in this field? These are all questions many women ask themselves. Come join us in the January session to discuss solutions to these questions.

In this session by Alicia you will learn about:


  • The past, present, and future for women in technology
  • How to close and retain the gender gap
  • How and Why to get girls involved in tech at an early age with TechGirlz!

Presented By Alicia Park
National Outreach Manager TechGirlz


Meet Alicia

"Alicia is an experienced educator, with a strong passion for Ed Tech, STEM and empowering young girls to explore the possibilities of working with technology. As National Outreach Manager for TechGirlz she works with volunteers and other organizations to help them bring the free technology based workshops (or TechShopz in a BoxTM, as they’ve coined them) to new communities across the U.S. She also manages the website blog, where you can check out the latest news on TechGirlz or read about their volunteers’ experiences with the organization!"
