Boosting Your Software Team's Productivity:

The SPACE Framework in Action

Measuring the productivity of engineering teams is challenging, and traditional DevOps metrics only focus on technical outcomes. Metrics like deployment frequency and mean time to restore a service are easy to measure but fail to provide a complete view of team performance, including factors like motivation and communication effectiveness. Engineering is a collaborative process that requires consideration of human and emotional aspects.

To address this, the creators of the DORA metrics developed the SPACE framework, which consists of five dimensions: Satisfaction & well-being, Performance, Activity, Communication & collaboration, and Efficiency & flow. The SPACE framework offers a more holistic approach to measuring developer productivity.
