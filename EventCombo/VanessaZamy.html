Well, you don't.One of the biggest myths about ‘the hustle’ is that it goes hand-in-hand with burnout and fatigue. As a consultant who helps busy women grow their businesses while still working, Vanessa knows that healthy time efficiency comes down to putting YOU first and creating quality over quantity.

The no nonsense, simplified way to peace, productivity, and prosperity


Our time is our most valuable resource, and in today’s fast-paced society, it’sall too easy to lose track of it in pursuit of our endless to-do’s. Vanessa Zamy will share better ways that ambitious, purpose-led business owners (from solopreneurs to leaders of big teams) can manage their time so they ‘hustle while breathing’ and optimize the synergy between their life and goals.

Small business owners who feel they don't have any time for themselves.
Solopreneurs sacrificing sleep or other pleasures to make things happen.
How to feel less exhausted, overwhelmed, and extended while doing it all. Remain motivated through the highs and lows of productivity.
Anyone growing a business who's thought about quitting their job so they can have more time.
Solopreneurs (teams of less than 3) who can't possibly fathom how to find the time to do all the things.
Entrepreneurs feeling overwhelmed with their business growth.
Solopreneurs stuck on how/what to do next.

Key Lessons:

How to juggle personal and professional obligations without sacrificing self.
How to prioritize and find the time for what is needed when the time is right. Hustle and breath feeling cool, calm, and collected.
Discover how to plan for success in any season, any pandemic, and political, social, economic turmoil. Implement an easy, simple way to define clear strategic priorities in your marketing and sales
Three main time principles to live by in life.

Meet Vanessa

A business expert, keynote speaker, and bestselling author of FINISH: The Solopreneur’s Guide To Getting Stuff Done. She is dedicated to helping full-time employees transition to entrepreneurship, time freedom, and financial freedom by growing their business while employed.

Since starting her business while employed in 2019, Vanessa was able to peacefully and calmly leave her six-figure day job in early 2021 and never look back. Through her consulting company, Your Vision's Catalyst, Vanessa Zamy works with high-achieving, corporate employees to increase their efficiency, productivity, and profitability so they can reduce overwhelm, and enjoy their life while growing their profit-producing, purpose-driven business.

Her motto: keep it simple, then keep it movin’! Vanessa has been featured in Fast Company and on the 2021 Women To Watch list by The Women In Business Club. Her background includes a Management Science and Engineering Degree from Stanford University, and strategic leadership roles at Fortune 500 companies.
